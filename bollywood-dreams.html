Bollywood Dreams

Follow    the    Crowd     Or    Stand    Out.

By Ferose War posted Nov 20, 2016

        Competition really is real tough,

        Real tough than you can think of


        Hurry up, lots more are in line 

        Lots & lots more wasted in wine...


Are you serious for your career in entertainment industry? Do you want to be the Next-Big Star?

well, of course you do.

But first ask some basic questions to self:

  • Have I got what it takes to be what I want to be?
  • What is my planning & strategy?
  • Do I know how to be what I want to be?
  • Based on the time I spend chasing my bollywood dream, where will I be in next, say 5 years?
  • I should be honest with myself. Am I being honest with myself? I can't live in false dreams when everday new people keep rushing to bollywood with their own bollywood dreams. What am I doing so that I won't be left behind?


However, we can't deny that around 90% of people chasing this Bollywood dream never reach it. Not that they dont have talent, they do of course. 

It might sound scary, but it's not. It's a force to push you to your dreams. Don't let this force deminish in false fame.


I used the word - False fame. "I am successful, I have done this n that things in bollywood, I am a star bla bla bla...". Well, if you are a star, why are you giving your introduction? why you putting down your profile in front of everybody? You clearly know that you are forcing yourself on people to accept you as a celebrity. If you are a celebrity, you don't need to give your introduction. People will know it without your need to tell them. Such I call as False fame.


False fame keeps you stuck in past but the future is ahead of you. Call yourself a Star/Celebrity when you reach the place where you always want to be at. No less No more 


Till you reach your goals, you of course need to appreciate yourself and your hardwork. But for your own self and never for the people. Beacuse when you try to prove your worth to other people, then you surely know you are lying to yourself, just to make your ownself happy.


You don't need other's appreciation to make youself happy. True Happiness come from within.


Or you have got what it takes to be a Star but you just need a chance? 


Well, chance... So its what you depend on?  Chance... waiting for your chance... waiting since how long? How are you sure it will come by? What if you don't get your chance? Is it the excuse that you will be giving yourself tomorrow for not reaching your goals? chance...  waiting... longing... Its all excuses, unless you accept what you get and be happy with what you are and keep the false fame way away from your doors.
